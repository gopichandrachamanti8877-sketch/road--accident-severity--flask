📘 Project Overview

Road traffic accidents are among the leading causes of fatalities worldwide. Early identification of accident severity enables emergency services to prioritize response, reduce rescue time, and improve patient survival rates.

This project presents an AI-powered Road Accident Severity Classification system using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). The system automatically classifies accident images into three severity levels:

  • Minor Impact
  • Substantial Impact
  • Critical Impact

🔬 Research Problem

Road traffic accidents are one of the leading causes of injuries and fatalities worldwide. Determining the severity of an accident quickly is critical for emergency response and resource allocation.

Traditional assessment methods rely on eyewitness reports or manual inspection, which may be slow, inconsistent, and prone to human error. This can delay emergency medical assistance and increase the risk of severe consequences.

This project investigates the use of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to automatically classify road accident images into three severity levels:

  • Minor Impact
  • Substantial Impact
  • Critical Impact

By automating the severity classification process, the proposed system aims to support faster emergency decision-making, improve response efficiency, and demonstrate the practical application of deep learning in intelligent transportation systems.

🎯 Research Objectives

The primary objective of this research is to develop an intelligent web-based system capable of automatically classifying the severity of road accidents from images using deep learning techniques.

  • Develop a CNN-based road accident severity classification model.
  • Compare the performance of Custom CNN, MobileNetV2, and EfficientNetB0 models.
  • Improve accident severity prediction accuracy using transfer learning.
  • Deploy the trained model as a user-friendly Flask web application.
